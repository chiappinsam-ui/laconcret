Welcome to L.A Concrete - Your Trusted Partners in Quality Construction.

Established with a singular vision, L.A Concrete was born from the collaboration of seasoned industry professionals who wanted to raise the standard of service in the Australian construction industry.

At L.A Concrete, we are driven by a steadfast commitment to honesty, hard work, and customer satisfaction. We strive to earn our place as your first choice for all concrete needs. Our friendly and dedicated team is here to assist you every step of the way, ensuring that your project vision becomes a tangible reality.

Anthony Di Rubbo has personally overseen the completion of more than 50,000 jobs over his extensive 35-year career. From complex commercial infrastructure to bespoke residential driveways, our team brings a wealth of knowledge and expertise to every project we undertake.

Our extensive list of services includes everything from concrete installation and finishing, to intricate repairs and maintenance. We understand that every project is unique, which is why we take the time to listen to your needs and customize a solution that fits your budget and timeline.
